This is an app I came up with to solve the dispute between my fiancee and I. She would sneakily pay, or I would. So, to solve the problem I made this app which randomly chooses a name using a random number generator. I have plans to upgrade it to where you can choose how many people are in your group to generate that many name fields. It's written in Java and I used Android Studio as the development environment.

If you'd like to download and try it, click the link: Who Pays?

This is the first Android app I made with Unity. I was pretty pleased with myself, and have recently done some updates to it to make sure it follows all proper coding standards for C#. The idea of it is to type your (or a random) weight in pounds and calculate it for different bodies in the Solar System.
